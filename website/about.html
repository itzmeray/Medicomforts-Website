About Us

RKR OMI MediComforts

MediComforts specializes in manufacturing disposable surgery packs, pre-designed drapes, and wraps for healthcare organizations. As a market leader, we focus on understanding the dynamic nature of the healthcare industry and delivering customized products tailored to our clients' needs within the promised timeframe.

Our range of products are:

  • Surgeon Gown
  • Patient Gown / OT Table Sheet / Hole Towel / Hole Drape
  • Disposable Bed Sheet and Pillow Covers
  • PPE Kit
  • General Surgery Drape Kit / Major / Minor
  • LSCS / Laparoscopic / Delivery Kits
  • HIV Kit / TKR / Ortho Kit

We also offer all kinds of customization based on customer requirements. Established in 2019, the company has its corporate office in Bangalore and branch office in Gadag. Our raw material suppliers are certified with ISO, CE, and AAMI standards. We provide sterilization reports for all batches.

Our Vision

Our vision is to deliver high-quality innovative products manufactured under highly sterile conditions. We aim to create a benchmark in the healthcare industry by being one of the prime suppliers of surgical disposables. We are committed to giving quality products at competitive prices, which is one of our top priorities.
