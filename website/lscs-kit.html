Comprehensive kit for lower segment caesarean section procedures.

Product Overview

The LSCS Kit by MediComforts is a comprehensive solution designed for lower segment caesarean section procedures. This kit includes all necessary sterile components to ensure a safe and efficient surgical environment for healthcare professionals.

Key Features

  • Sterile and single-use components
  • Includes adhesive sheets and cautery bag for enhanced functionality
  • Designed for comprehensive coverage and infection control
  • High-quality, absorbent materials
  • Easy-to-use and dispose of
